Wiz Khalifa

Origin

Cameron Jibril Thomaz (born September 8, 1987), known professionally as Wiz Khalifa, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and actor. He released his debut album, Show and Prove, in 2006, and signed to Warner Bros. Records in 2007. His Eurodance-influenced single, "Say Yeah", received urban radio airplay, charting on the Rhythmic Top 40 and Hot Rap Tracks charts in 2008, becoming his first minor hit. His best known songs are "Black and Yellow" "We Dem Boyz", "KK" (feat Juicy J), "No Sleep", "Roll Up", and "See You Again". His also appears in Mac & Devin Go to High School alongside rapper Snoop Dogg.
